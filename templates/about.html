Rental Policies

1. Account & Verification

Every renter must register and log in before booking. This keeps our platform secure and ensures every booking is tied to a verified name, phone number and email.

2. Booking & Pricing

Prices are calculated based on the daily rate of the vehicle, the number of rental days, and the selected destination. A 5% advance via eSewa secures the booking; the remainder is paid on pickup.

3. Editing & Cancellation

You can edit or cancel your booking any time from "My Bookings" — but only until our team verifies it. Once verified, changes must go through our support team.

4. Availability

Vehicle availability is live — a vehicle shows "Booked" automatically the moment another customer's booking is confirmed, and becomes "Available" again automatically if that booking is cancelled.

5. Vehicle Condition

All vehicles are inspected before handover. Renters are expected to return vehicles in the same condition, with any damage reported and assessed at return.
